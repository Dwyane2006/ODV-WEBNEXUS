About Us

Our Story, Mission & Vision

Our History

The 𝙊𝙧𝙙𝙚𝙧 𝙤𝙛 𝘿𝙖 𝙑𝙞𝙣𝙘𝙞 (𝙊𝘿𝙑) was founded in 2024 by Mr. Louie Jay Torralba Estrella, together with the Founding Grand Masters, Mr. Christian Jay Barbadillo Gumapac, Mr. Ian Albert Secopito Dela Peña, and Mr. Emanuel Tambong Monte Jr., and the Founding Master, Mr. Fhiel Andrey Morales.

They envisioned an exclusive brotherhood of men who embody the values of wisdom and knowledge, drawing inspiration from the intellectual legacy of Leonardo da Vinci, whose timeless contributions to art, science, and philosophy continue to influence the modern world. Established with the mission to empower the youth, serve communities, and protect the environment, the Order sought to cultivate unity, discipline, and service among its members.

The history of the Order began on September 18, 2024, when the first ODV Group Chat was created, marking the initial gathering of aspirants who shared the same goals of unity, growth, and transformation. On the same day, the ODV Orientation was conducted, laying the foundation for the organization’s culture of discipline and brotherhood. The following day, September 19, 2024, the first ODV Session was officially held, where founding members committed themselves to uphold the principles of the Order.

From September 2024 to January 2025, sixteen young men aspired to become the founding members, undergoing rigorous training, seminars, sessions, and project implementations that prepared them for the challenges of true Da Vincians.

Our Purpose

Mission

“We, the members of the Order of Da Vinci, united by the values of leadership, honor, valor, service, and brotherhood, pledge to cultivate visionary men committed to personal growth, ethical leadership, and meaningful impact. Inspired by Leonardo da Vinci’s legacy, we uphold excellence, unity, and resilience through learning and purposeful action. Bound by loyalty, respect, and mutual support, we stand united in service and integrity. Do hereby ordain and promulgate this Constitution."

Vision

"To cultivate a brotherhood of visionary thinkers, creators, and leaders who embody curiosity, artistry, growth, and discovery. The Order of Da Vinci is committed to advancing excellence in knowledge, fostering a spirit of collaboration, and driving transformative actions that contribute to the progress and well-being of society."

Objectives

The Order of Da Vinci is dedicated to developing strong, ethical leaders who inspire positive change, navigate innovation, and embody camaraderie. With brotherhood at its heart, the organization fosters deep bonds of unity and mutual support among its members. Its objectives focus on preparing college men to face life’s challenges by equipping them with the skills, knowledge, and sense of belonging necessary to thrive. The Order upholds honor, cultivates integrity, and inspires responsibility, while encouraging members to live with valor, resilience, and determination in overcoming obstacles. Through continuous learning opportunities, the Order empowers members to contribute meaningfully to society. By emphasizing collaboration and mutual growth, it builds a supportive network that strengthens unity and fuels collective success. In adherence to the Rule of Law and the preservation of peace and order, these objectives ensure that the Order of Da Vinci shapes individuals who are ready to lead, value enduring brotherhood, and leave a lasting, positive impact on the world.
