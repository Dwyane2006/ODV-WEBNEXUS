Our Purpose

Mission

To cultivate a brotherhood of visionary thinkers, creators, and leaders who embody curiosity, artistry, growth, and discovery. The Order of Da Vinci is committed to advancing excellence in knowledge, fostering a spirit of collaboration, and driving transformative actions that contribute to the progress and well-being of society.

Vision

To be a Transformative and Holistic Brotherhood that ignites innovation, cultivates creativity, and empowers visionaries to excel in art, science, and leadership while shaping a future that is sustainable, inclusive, and filled with limitless possibilities.

Core Values

Service True service begins with oneself—cultivating discipline, compassion, and integrity that radiate outward. It reflects a deep commitment to uplift communities, inspire others, and contribute to the greater good. Honor embodies integrity, dignity, and the responsibility of upholding the ideals of the Order. Valor signifies courage in facing challenges and resilience in overcoming adversity. Leadership is the pursuit of excellence in service—guiding others with vision, wisdom, and accountability. It is about transforming minds, nurturing potential, and building new leaders who will continue the legacy of purpose and impact. Above all, Brotherhood binds members together in loyalty, unity, and lifelong solidarity, forming the foundation of true Da Vincians.
