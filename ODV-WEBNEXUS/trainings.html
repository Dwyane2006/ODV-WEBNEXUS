Aspirants Training Program

A structured journey from Preliminaries to Acceptance

The Aspirants Training Program guides every candidate through a structured journey — from the Preliminaries and Service Stage, through the Honor and Valor Stages, to the Brotherhood Stage and Acceptance Ceremony.

Along the way, aspirants build leadership, first aid readiness, and public speaking confidence through real sessions like "I am a Leader," "Basic Life Support and Basic First Aid," and "The Art of Voice," guided by ODV Members, Grand Masters, and Aspirants together.
