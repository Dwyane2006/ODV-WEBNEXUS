Founded in 2024 by a group of visionary scholars and innovators, the Order of Da Vinci was established to honor the spirit of Leonardo da Vinci — a man whose curiosity knew no bounds and whose work transcended disciplines.

Order of Da Vinci (ODV) is a brotherhood that transforms minds and builds leaders through leadership, honor, valor, and service. It empowers young individuals to become visionary leaders and lead change in their communities and beyond.

Today, we are proud to have over 20 active members united by a shared commitment to excellence, creativity, and service.

Our Purpose

Mission

“To cultivate a community of visionary thinkers, creators, and leaders who embody the essence of curiosity, artistry, and discovery. The Order of Da Vinci is committed to advancing excellence in knowledge, fostering a spirit of collaboration, and driving transformative actions that contribute to the progress and well-being of society.

Vision

"To be a Transformative and Holistic Youth Brotherhood that ignites innovation, cultivates creativity, and empowers visionaries to excel in art, science, and leadership while shaping a future that is sustainable, inclusive, and filled with limitless possibilities." Brother 3: The Order of D

Objectives

The Order of Da Vinci is dedicated to developing strong, ethical leaders who inspire positive change, drive innovation, and embody camaraderie. With brotherhood at its heart, the organization fosters deep bonds of unity and mutual support among its members. Its objectives focus on preparing college men to face life’s challenges by equipping them with the skills, knowledge, and sense of belonging necessary to thrive. The Order upholds honor, cultivates integrity, and inspires responsibility, while encouraging members to live with valor, resilience, and determination in overcoming obstacles. Through continuous learning opportunities, the Order empowers members tocontribute meaningfully to society. By emphasizing collaboration and mutual growth, it builds a supportive network that strengthens unity and fuels collective success. In adherence to the Rule of Law and the preservation of peace and order, these objectives ensure that the Order of Da Vinci shapes individuals who are ready to lead, value enduring brotherhood, and leave a lasting, positive impact on the world.
