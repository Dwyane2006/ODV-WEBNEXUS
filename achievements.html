Our Achievements

Milestones That Define Excellence

Awards & Recognitions

Celebrating decades of innovation and impact

January 24, 2025

Registered Student Organization (RSO) Accreditation

The Order of Da Vinci was officially recognized as a Registered Student Organization of the Agusan del Sur State College of Agriculture and Technology for A.Y. 2025-2026, granted under the Office of Student Affairs.

March 20, 2025

The Radiance — Official Publication, Vol. 1

Conceived under founding President Ramil Paciano Atog, The Radiance launched as ODV's official publication, documenting the Order's founding, its first members, and the Da Vincian way of life.

May 5, 2025

National Youth Commission YORP Registration

The National Youth Commission officially approved ODV for YORP registration under Republic Act No. 8044, granting the organization full rights under the 2018 Revitalized Youth Organization Registration Guidelines.

June 2025

The Radiance — Official Publication, Vol. 2

The second volume of The Radiance continued the Order's mission of documenting its milestones while reflecting its core values: Service, Honor, Valor, Leadership, and Brotherhood.

Year 3

Top 5 Finalist — Tertiary Student Leaders Confab on Human Rights

ODV placed as a Top 5 Finalist in the Commission on Human Rights – CARAGA and Friedrich Naumann Foundation's Tertiary Student Leaders Confab on Human Rights, earning a PHP 5,000 cash award alongside Rank 4 in Project Poster and Rank 6 in the Human Rights Fair project pitching.

Ongoing

ODV Milestones

Top 20 Finalist for the Most Outstanding Org. for the People Award (SDSN Youth Ten Outstanding UN SDG Awards), an Accredited Organization under the UN Sustainable Development Solutions Network Youth Philippines, and a Re-Accredited Organization with ASSCAT.

2025

ODV Goes Digital

ODV expanded its reach across Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and YouTube, surpassing 2.2K+ followers, 500K+ views, 12.6K+ interactions, and 68K+ reach to share its causes and advocacies in the digital world.

A.Y. 2025-2026

Membership Development Award for Student Organization

The Order of Da Vinci was presented a Plaque of Recognition by the Agusan del Sur State College of Agriculture and Technology for the Membership Development Award for Student Organization, in acknowledgment of its dedicated efforts in initiating and implementing student programs and activities.
